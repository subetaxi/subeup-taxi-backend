Before the trip

Getting a safe ride

Safe pickups The SubeUp app automatically finds your location to provide door-to-door service. That means you stay safe and comfortable wherever you are until your driver arrives.

Open to everyone, everywhere All ride requests are blindly matched with the closest available driver. So there is no discrimination based on race, gender, or destination.

Driver profiles When you’re matched with a driver, you’ll see their name, license plate number, photo, and rating—so you know who’s picking you up ahead of time. And even after the trip, you’re able to contact your driver if you left something behind.

On tripGetting to your destination

Share your ETA Once your driver has picked you up, share your ETA with your friends and family so they can follow your route and know when to expect you.

Always on the map Follow your trip in real-time so you always know where you are. And if you use SubeUpPOOL, you’ll know exactly who’s riding with you.

After the trip

Always here for you

Anonymous feedbackAfter every trip, you can rate the driver and provide anonymous feedback about your ride. We review all feedback because our goal is to make every ride a great experience.

24/7 supportOur support team is always ready to respond to any questions you may have about your trip and help you retrieve lost items.

Rapid responseOur specially-trained incident response teams are available around the clock to handle any urgent concerns that arise.

In the App and OfflineImproving experiences with technology

Substitute phone numbers

In many locations around the world, SubeUp uses technology that anonymizes phone numbers to keep contact details confidential. So when you and your driver need to contact each other, your personal information stays private.

Always on the map

GPS data is logged for every trip so we know where and when you’re riding and who’s behind the wheel. It also helps us ensure that drivers are taking the best route every time.

Assisting law enforcement

In cases where local law enforcement provides us with valid legal process, we provide them with useful data to help in their investigations.
